Tense Translation Patterns

Present: she [verbs], e.g. she walks

Imperfect: he was [verb]ing, he used to [verb], he kept [verb]ing; e.g. he was running

Future: she will [verb], e.g. she will rejoice

Perfect: he [verb]ed, e.g. he sailed

Pluperfect: she had [verb]ed, e.g. she had hoped

Future perfect: he will have [verb]ed, e.g. he will have arrived
